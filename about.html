Our Story

WHY DAASA EXISTS.

South India has 600 million+ vernacular language speakers. Kannada. Tamil. Telugu. Brilliant creators, emerging brands, and growing businesses — all producing content. And almost none of them had access to a studio that truly understood their language, culture, and ambitions.

Durgaprasad Karunadu saw this gap firsthand. The existing options were expensive agencies that treated vernacular content as an afterthought, or individual freelancers with no reliability or consistency.

Daasa was the answer: a full-stack content studio built natively for South India, powered by AI to bring down cost without compromising quality, and staffed by people who live and breathe Kannada, Tamil, and Telugu content.

We launched in Bengaluru in 2026. Broadcast contracts. Startup subscriptions. Creator partnerships. Every project teaches us something. Every delivery makes us faster. We're just getting started.
