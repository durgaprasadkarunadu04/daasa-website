Full-Stack Services

WHAT
WE DO.

Eight professional services. One studio. Powered by AI, delivered by people who understand South Indian content culture.

01
SCRIPTING & IDEATION

Great content starts with a great script. Our AI-assisted scripting team develops story concepts, shooting scripts, and social copy — before a single camera turns on. We work in Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, and English.

  • Long-form & short-form video scripts
  • Social media content calendars
  • Brand story & narrative development
  • Vernacular scripting in KN / TA / TE / EN
  • AI-accelerated concept generation
02
VIDEO PRODUCTION

Professional-grade video production — studio and on-location across Bengaluru. Our team handles everything from setup to wrap, so you just show up and perform.

  • 4K studio shoots & on-location production
  • Professional lighting & sound design
  • Teleprompter & crew support
  • Multi-camera event coverage
  • Broadcast-standard technical specs
03
POST-PRODUCTION

AI-accelerated editing with human artistry. We handle every element of post — from cut to delivery — fast enough for social, polished enough for broadcast.

  • Multi-track video editing & assembly
  • AI-assisted rough cut & pacing
  • Professional colour grading
  • Motion graphics & title sequences
  • Sound design, mixing & music licensing
  • Subtitles in KN / TA / TE / EN
04
SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGEMENT

Your content doesn't stop performing when it's posted. Daasa manages the full lifecycle — from scheduling to community engagement to monthly analytics reports.

  • Platform strategy & content calendar
  • YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn
  • Community management & engagement
  • Hashtag research & SEO optimisation
  • Monthly analytics & growth reports

WEBSITE DEVELOPMENT

Your website is your most permanent piece of content. Daasa builds brand websites, portfolio pages, and landing pages — optimised for search and conversion from day one.

  • Brand websites & portfolio sites
  • Product landing pages
  • SEO-optimised from build
  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • Handover with editable CMS

VERNACULAR CONTENT

600M+ South Indians consume content in Kannada, Tamil, and Telugu. Daasa produces natively — not translated. Real scripts by people who live and breathe these languages.

  • Vernacular social media content

  • Regional talent network

FREELANCER NETWORK

Need specific talent for a specific shoot? Daasa's vetted freelancer network gives you access to South India's best independent creatives — without the overhead of an agency.

  • Kannada / Tamil / Telugu voice artists
  • Animators & graphic designers
  • Per-project or ongoing placement

BROADCAST EDITING

The deadline is non-negotiable on broadcast. Daasa delivers broadcast-ready post-production in 5–7 days for TV events — reality shows, award ceremonies, cultural galas. Trusted by Zee Kannada, Suvarna TV, and Colors Kannada.
