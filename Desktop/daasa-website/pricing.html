FAQ

QUESTIONS ANSWERED.

Is there a minimum contract?

We ask for a 3-month minimum on all subscription plans — enough time to see real content momentum. After that, cancel with 30 days notice.

What if I need more videos than my plan includes?

Top-up videos are available at ₹3,500–₹6,000 per video depending on complexity. We'll quote you before proceeding.

Do you shoot in studios or on location?

Both. We have access to partner studios in Bengaluru and can shoot on-location across the city. Location shoots may attract a small travel surcharge outside city limits.

Can I mix services from different plans?

Yes. All services can be purchased a la carte. Contact us and we'll build a custom scope that matches your exact needs and budget.

How fast do you deliver?

Standard turnaround is 5–7 working days post-shoot. Pro and Premium clients get priority queue (3–5 days). Broadcast projects are guaranteed in 5–7 days.

Do you work with brands outside Bengaluru?

Yes — scripting, post-production, social media, and website services are fully remote-friendly. Production shoots can be arranged with local crew in other cities on request.
