Full-Stack Services

WHAT WE DO.

Eight professional services. One studio. Powered by AI, delivered by people who understand South Indian content culture.

✍️
01
SCRIPTING & IDEATION

Great content starts with a great script. Our team develops story concepts, shooting scripts, and social copy — in Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, and English.

  • Long-form & short-form video scripts
  • Social media content calendars
  • Brand story & narrative development
  • Vernacular scripting in KN / TA / TE / EN
🎬
02
VIDEO PRODUCTION

Professional-grade video production — studio and on-location across Bengaluru. We handle everything from setup to wrap.

  • 4K studio shoots & on-location production
  • Professional lighting & sound design
  • Multi-camera event coverage
  • Broadcast-standard technical specs
✂️
03
POST-PRODUCTION

End-to-end editing with human artistry. We handle every element of post — from cut to delivery — fast enough for social, polished enough for broadcast.

  • Multi-track video editing & assembly
  • Professional colour grading
  • Motion graphics & title sequences
  • Sound design, mixing & music
  • Subtitles in KN / TA / TE / EN
📱
04
SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGEMENT

Your content doesn't stop performing when it's posted. Daasa manages the full lifecycle — scheduling, community engagement, and monthly analytics.

  • Platform strategy & content calendar
  • YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn
  • Community management & engagement
  • Monthly analytics & growth reports
🌐
05
WEBSITE DEVELOPMENT

Your website is your most permanent piece of content. Daasa builds brand websites and landing pages — optimised for search and conversion from day one.

  • Brand websites & portfolio sites
  • Product landing pages
  • SEO-optimised from build
  • Mobile-first responsive design
🗣️
06
VERNACULAR CONTENT

600M+ South Indians consume content in Kannada, Tamil, and Telugu. Daasa produces natively — not translated. Real scripts by people who live these languages.

  • Native scripting in KN, TA, TE
  • Culturally-aware storytelling
  • Vernacular social media content
  • Dubbed & subtitled versions
🤝
07
FREELANCER NETWORK

Access to South India's best independent creatives — vetted, reliable, and ready to go — without the overhead of a full agency.

  • Videographers & cinematographers
  • Video editors & motion designers
  • Kannada / Tamil / Telugu voice artists
  • Animators & graphic designers
📺
08
BROADCAST EDITING

The deadline is non-negotiable on broadcast. Daasa delivers broadcast-ready post-production in 5–7 days for TV events. Trusted by Zee Kannada, Suvarna TV, and Colors Kannada.

  • Reality show event editing
  • Award ceremony coverage
  • Broadcast-standard delivery in 5–7 days
  • Per-event or annual retainer
