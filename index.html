FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS.

Common questions about digital marketing, content creation, and working with Daasa — answered directly.

Digital marketing is promoting your brand, products, or services through online channels — including social media, search engines, email, video, and websites. It allows you to reach the right audience at the right time with measurable results.
Content marketing builds trust, drives organic traffic, and converts audiences into customers. Businesses that publish consistent, quality content generate significantly more leads than those that don't — and at a fraction of the cost of traditional advertising.
Organic social media growth typically takes 3–6 months to show meaningful results. Paid campaigns can drive traffic within days. Daasa focuses on building sustainable growth — not vanity metrics — with monthly performance reports to track real progress.
SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) is the process of improving your website so it ranks higher on Google and other search engines. Higher rankings mean more free, organic traffic. Daasa builds all websites SEO-optimised from day one — not as an afterthought.
Short-form vertical video (Reels, Shorts, YT Shorts) dominates engagement. But long-form documentary-style content and brand storytelling drive trust and conversions. The best strategy combines both. Daasa creates content tailored to each platform and audience.
If your audience is in South India, absolutely yes. Over 600 million people consume content in vernacular languages — and they engage far more with native-language content than with translated versions. Daasa produces vernacular content natively, not by translation.
Organic marketing (SEO, social media, content) builds long-term audience and trust without direct ad spend. Paid marketing (Google Ads, Meta Ads) delivers faster reach but stops when you stop paying. The best strategy uses both — Daasa helps you find the right balance.
AI accelerates our scripting, rough cut editing, social caption generation, and performance analysis. This reduces cost and turnaround time significantly. Every AI output is reviewed and refined by our human creative team — so quality is never compromised.
