FAQ — Digital Marketing

QUESTIONS WE GET ASKED A LOT.

Digital marketing promotes your brand through online channels — social media, search engines, email, video, and websites. It lets you reach the right audience at the right time with measurable, trackable results.
Content marketing builds trust and drives organic traffic. Businesses that publish consistent, quality content generate significantly more leads than those that don't — at a fraction of the cost of traditional advertising.
Organic social media growth typically takes 3–6 months to show meaningful results. Paid campaigns can drive traffic within days. Daasa focuses on sustainable growth with monthly performance reports.
SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) improves your website's ranking on Google. Higher rankings mean more free, organic traffic. Daasa builds all websites SEO-optimised from day one — not as an afterthought.
Short-form vertical video (Reels, Shorts) dominates engagement. Long-form brand storytelling drives trust and conversions. The best strategy combines both — Daasa creates content tailored to each platform.
If your audience is in South India, absolutely yes. Over 600 million people consume vernacular content — and they engage far more with native-language content than with English translations.
Organic marketing (SEO, social, content) builds long-term audience and trust without direct ad spend. Paid marketing delivers faster reach but stops when you stop paying. The best strategy uses both.
AI accelerates scripting, rough cut editing, social caption generation, and performance analysis. This reduces cost and turnaround time. Every AI output is reviewed and refined by our human creative team.
